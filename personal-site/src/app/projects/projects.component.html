Recipe Book

This recipe book was created through the course "Angular - The Complete Guide (2020 Edition)" on Udemy. In this project, you are able to create your own recipe book by creating an account. You are able to create recipes by adding and mixing ingredients as well as place ingredients into a shopping list. If you are to logout, your recipes will be preserved and ready for your next login. This project demonstrates basic concepts such as Components, Directives, Observables, HTTP Requests, Pipes, Authentication and Route Protection. Firebase was used as the backend to mimic authentication and a realtime database.
